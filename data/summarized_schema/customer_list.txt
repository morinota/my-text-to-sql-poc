The "customer_list" table contains data related to customers, specifically including the following columns: ID, name, address, zip_code, phone, city, country, notes, and SID. 

This table can be utilized to store customer contact information, providing a basis for customer relationship management. It allows for the tracking of customer locations, as it includes address and ZIP code details, which can assist in geographical analysis or service area identification. The phone field enables the possibility of outreach for customer service or marketing purposes.

Potential use cases for this table include:

- Identifying the distribution of customers across different cities or countries.
- Analyzing customer demographics based on location data.
- Managing customer communications and recording pertinent notes regarding interactions or preferences.
- Segmenting customers for targeted marketing strategies based on their geographical locations and characteristics. 
- Assessing the customer base by reviewing the number of customers within specific ZIP code areas.
