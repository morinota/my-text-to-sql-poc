The "sales_by_store" table contains information related to sales performance across different stores. It has the following columns: 

- **store_id**: A unique identifier for each store.
- **store**: The name of the store.
- **manager**: The name of the store's manager.
- **total_sales**: The total sales amount for each store represented as a decimal value.

This table can be used to analyze sales data at the store level. Potential use cases include:

- Determining which stores have the highest or lowest total sales.
- Comparing sales performance between different stores.
- Evaluating the impact of store management on sales figures by analyzing total sales per manager.
- Identifying trends in sales over time if combined with date-related data.
- Assisting in inventory management and stock allocation based on sales performance at specific stores.
