The "film_text" table contains information related to films, specifically their IDs, titles, and descriptions. The columns include:

- **film_id** (INTEGER): This is the primary key that uniquely identifies each film in the table.
- **title** (VARCHAR(255)): This column holds the names of the films and cannot be null.
- **description** (TEXT): This column contains detailed descriptions of the films, providing context or background information about each film.

Potential use cases for this table include answering questions about specific films based on their titles or descriptions, conducting analysis on film content, generating lists of films based on keywords in their descriptions, and examining trends in film titles or themes. It can also be useful for creating film databases, supporting search functionalities, and providing information for film-related applications or websites.
